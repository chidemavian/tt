These are the major steps you should follow, if you want to make the most of the report section
1. The Vaults menu gives you the total current deposits at any particular time
2. Use the Performance menu to verify the accrued amount, in terms of sales for each credit officers.
3. If you wish to know your profit level at any time, use the Profit link
4. Sometimes, there may be incomplete transactions. To view what these might be, use the Unapprovals link
5. To know the total amount taken out at a particular period, the withdrawals link will be handy
